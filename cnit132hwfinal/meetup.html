Code Self Study is a self–directed meetup group based in Berkeley that meets twice per week:

  • Wednesday's 10:00am – 1:00pm @ Berkeley South Branch Library
  • Saturday's 2:00pm – 5:00pm @ Au Coquelet

Most sessions are self–directed without a fixed plan or leader. During a meetup, members can work on a project independently or partner up for pair programming. Optional activities can be organized by an organizer from the group. Experienced programers in the group can offer mentorship for learning. The group objectives are to learn new skills, meet friendly creative people, form study groups, provide networking opportunities and assistance in job placement. At Code Self Study the array of languages that members work with cover the whole spectrum ranging from Python, JavaScript, Rust, Java, Scheme, Racket, Haskell, Clojure, Common Lisp, C, C++, C#, F#, Lua, Elixir, Erlang, Elm, Purescript, Ruby, Scala, PHP, Go, R, HTML/CSS, Octave, SQL and more.
